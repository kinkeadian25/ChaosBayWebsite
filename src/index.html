About Us

About Chaos Bay

At Chaos Bay, we're all about crafting exceptional beers that showcase the spirit of our Bremerton, Washington community. Using only the finest ingredients and time-honored brewing methods, we create unique and flavorful brews that are loved by locals and visitors alike. But we're more than just a brewery.

Our passion for good food is just as strong as our love for great beer. Our kitchen serves up a rotating menu of delicious bites and full meals, all made from scratch using locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you're stopping in for a cold pint or a tasty bite, we can't wait to welcome you to Chaos Bay.

"Quote from owner"

Justin Time CEO-Founder

Our Event Service

Bring your events to us

At Chaos Bay Brewing, we love bringing people together to celebrate and have a great time. That's why we offer two unique event spaces to host your next gathering. Our concert stage area is perfect for live music performances, featuring a state-of-the-art sound system and plenty of space for your guests to dance and mingle.

And for a more intimate setting, our large private room is ideal for birthdays, corporate events, or any other occasion where you want to enjoy great food and drinks with your closest friends and family. Plus, with our talented in-house kitchen team, we can create a custom menu tailored to your specific needs. So whether you're looking to rock out on our concert stage or relax in our private room, Chaos Bay Brewing has the perfect space for your next event.
